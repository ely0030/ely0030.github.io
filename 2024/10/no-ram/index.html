    No RAM

    October 9, 2024

    My short term memory is abysmal

    I came up with 3 good ideas, and a moment later I can’t remember any of them.

    I had to really to cling onto this idea (having no short term memory) and it was a struggle to not forget and jump something else.

    Even writing this right now I want to think of a million other ideas and move on to a different subject.

    Like, if I don’t watch it, my mind will just go blank and go “i need to watch youtube”.

    …

    How can I do literally anything without having a short term memory, or attention span?

    I think I’m realizing right now that I literally don’t have an attention span.

    Remedies #

    How do I remedy this?

    1. Stop consuming short form content The more you jump from topic to topic, the more primed your brain becomes to operate that way.

    You need to seize anything that makes you jump from topic to topic.

    1. Engage in activities that engage your attention span This could be:
    • Reading
    • Long movies
    • Attempting to focus on one thing

    Implications of ADHD #

    So a very salient example is that this lack of being able to hold onto things impacts every task or action that you do in your every day life.

    A very salient example of this is {my dad}

    {my dad} can’t even hold onto what the other person has said in a conversation, and just acts on the whims of whatever thought arises in {his} brain.

    This makes {him} insufferable to have conversations with. This comes across as incredibly rude, and it’s a massive drag to talk to {him}.

    {He} can’t even help it though. I don’t think it’s on purpose, but it doesn’t really matter if in practicality you’re useless to have a conversation with.

    Notes #

    Writing this file I have to struggle really really fucking hard to keep my mind on this subject matter. There’s an invisible force pulling me towards literally anything else.

    If there’s a lightning rod (read: distraction) nearby I can’t seem to hold my focus on keeping my focus on this topic to write here at all. Let alone read it again.

    It’s simply not interesting enough.
